Rules

Each player play after one another. To play, the player moves his pawn to the country displayed on the card, if he guess the country correctly he get one point.

Roll a dice :
  • 1: give the population of the country
  • 2: give the capital of the country
  • 3: give the languages of the country
  • 4: give the currency of the country
  • 5: give the name of a random landmark amoung the 4 displayed on the card
  • 6: ask another player to read the definition of a dish or drink and give its name
If the player win he the card is withdrawn and the player get a point
If the country of the next card can be reach by land for the player current position it plays again if not it is the next player turn.

The player that first reach 20 points win the game.
